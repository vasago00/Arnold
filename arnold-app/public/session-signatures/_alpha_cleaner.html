Session Signature — Background Remover v3

Some "transparent" PNGs from Gemini actually have the transparency-checkerboard rendered as opaque grayscale pixels, not a real alpha channel. This tool detects the background colors automatically (by sampling the 4 corners) and removes any pixel matching them — turning the fake-transparent areas into actually-transparent pixels.

How to use: drop your 6 PNGs. The tool samples the corner colors automatically (shown as swatches under each preview). Tune Color tolerance if some background pixels survive (raise it) or figure pixels are getting eaten (lower it). Default 35 should work for most.
