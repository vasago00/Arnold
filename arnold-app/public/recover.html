How to recover your data

Your Arnold data is stored in the browser's localStorage, which is tied to the exact port number. If Arnold was running on port 5173 and then switched to 5174, the data stays on 5173.

Step 1: Open this page on each port to find your data:
  • localhost:5173/recover.html
  • localhost:5174/recover.html
  • localhost:5175/recover.html

Step 2: When you find the port with data, click "Download Backup".

Step 3: Go to the port where Arnold is running, open this recovery page there, and click "Restore from Backup".
