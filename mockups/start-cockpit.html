Why this is the right bar

It reads top-to-bottom like a flight deck: state → systems → mission → cautions. You synthesize readiness from a coordinated cluster, not a label — and there's genuinely *more* signal here than today, held legible by structure.

PRI

Primary cluster, not a verdict

Three readiness rings (HRV / sleep / recovery) + four micro-gauges (load A:C in its optimal band, recovery %, today's demand, strain room). You read the gestalt — "fresh, in-band, but under-recovered" — in one scan.

SYS

Systems row

Activity / Nutrition / Body, each with sub-readouts + a trend line. The engine instruments.

MSN

Mission band

Today's session, the next race countdown, week trajectory — your flight plan, glanceable.

CTN

Master caution

Only what's off lights up (sleep, protein, recovery). Status-by-exception is what lets the panel be dense without flooding — your eye goes straight to the ambers.
