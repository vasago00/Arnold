Bottom-nav spine

Five slots: Today · EdgeIQ · Daily · Calendar · More. More holds Body (Core/Labs), Stack (supplements), Profile, and a Plan glance. Swipe between primary tabs; Coach is ambient, not a tab.

Density follows the job

Today = medium (hero + why + plan + today's wins). EdgeIQ = high governed density. Daily = detail + reward. Calendar = scheduling. Each fills the screen — no dead gutters.

Color = exceptions

Most values neutral; amber/red only on off-target cells (sleep, cal/protein, recovery, carb ring). The signal pops out of the field.

Everything reflects the plan

Plan (web) is the source; Today shows today's slice, Calendar shows the schedule, Daily shows what you did against it. One plan, surfaced everywhere.
